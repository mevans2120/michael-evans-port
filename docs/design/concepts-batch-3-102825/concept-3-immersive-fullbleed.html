Concept 3: Immersive Full-Bleed Hero
Strategy: Bold, dramatic approach that creates maximum impact through full-viewport immersive experience. Animated gradient mesh and grid pattern create depth and motion, while centered content with prominent metrics commands attention. The full-bleed design feels premium and confident.

Strengths: Most visually striking, creates strong first impression, feels premium/high-end, good for portfolio differentiation, centered layout works well on all screen sizes
Tradeoffs: Potentially overwhelming, requires careful balance to maintain readability, may distract from content if not executed carefully, uses more visual real estate before showing other work

The full-bleed hero creates an immersive experience that fills the entire viewport. The animated gradient mesh (slowly scaling and rotating) creates subtle motion and depth, while the grid pattern adds technical sophistication. The dark overlay ensures text remains readable against the animated background. Centered content with large typography creates a bold statement, and the scroll indicator invites exploration. Multiple metrics displayed prominently establish credibility.
