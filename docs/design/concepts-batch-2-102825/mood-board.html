Mood Board: Featured Case Studies

Design direction for homepage featured work section, aligned with narrative scroll case study aesthetic

Color Palette

Dark, atmospheric backgrounds with vibrant purple accents. High contrast for readability.

Background Dark #0a0a14
Background Deeper #050510
Primary Purple hsl(280, 100%, 80%)
Secondary Purple hsl(276, 100%, 75%)
Text Primary #fafafa
Text Secondary #d0d0d0
Usage: Purple gradients for headlines and accent elements. Dark backgrounds create atmospheric depth. Light text ensures readability. Avoid pure white (#ffffff) for softer feel.

Typography

Two fonts working in harmony: DM Sans for structure, Crimson Pro for elegance.

Virgin America: First Responsive Airline
DM Sans / 48px / Weight 600 / Line-height 1.2 — Display headlines
Reimagining Airline Booking
DM Sans / 32px / Weight 500 / Line-height 1.3 — Section headings
Most of Virgin America's revenue came from business travelers—frequent flyers who pay premium prices and book last-minute. But leisure travelers, who book 2-3 times per year, were struggling with the complex purchase flow.
Crimson Pro / 18px / Weight 300 / Line-height 1.7 — Body text
Rationale: DM Sans provides geometric precision for UI elements and headings. Crimson Pro adds editorial elegance for longer-form content. Together they balance professionalism with approachability.

Layout Patterns

Three strategic approaches for displaying featured case studies on the homepage.

Minimal Cards
Large typography, generous spacing, vertical stacking. Information revealed on hover. Safe, refined approach that works on all devices.
Hero Image Tiles
Visual-first with large screenshots or graphics. Text overlays on hover. Bold, cinematic approach for showcasing work visually.
Horizontal Scroll
Wide cards that scroll horizontally like streaming services. Each card shows more detail. Experimental, immersive browsing experience.

Interactive Elements

Smooth transitions and micro-interactions that feel premium without being distracting.

Hover to experience interactions

Card Hover Effect
Subtle scale and glow
Interaction principles: 200-300ms transitions, cubic-bezier easing, subtle scale/glow effects on hover, clear affordances for clickable elements.

Spacing Scale

Generous whitespace creates breathing room and premium feel.

XS - 8px
SM - 16px
MD - 24px
LG - 40px
XL - 64px
2XL - 96px
Usage: XL+ spacing between major sections, MD for component padding, SM for tight groupings. More is more — generous whitespace = premium feel.

Atmospheric Effects

Subtle blur orbs create depth without overwhelming content.

Content floats above atmospheric effects
Blur orbs create depth, never distract
Implementation: Fixed position blur orbs with 100px+ blur radius, 0.15 opacity, positioned asymmetrically. Content always has z-index above effects.

Design Philosophy

These concepts prioritize content clarity over decoration, generous spacing over density, and smooth interactions over flashy animations. The dark theme with purple accents creates sophistication without feeling heavy. Large typography and breathing room signal confidence in the work.
