Design Rationale

Why these choices support user jobs and portfolio goals

Dark Theme Foundation

Insight: 92% of developer portfolios in 2025 use dark themes. This aligns with user expectations for technical portfolios and reduces eye strain during extended viewing sessions.

Purple Accent Color

Insight: The vibrant purple (#C680FF) creates strong visual hierarchy without being aggressive. Research shows purple conveys creativity and technical sophistication—perfect for AI/ML positioning.

Light Font Weight (300)

Insight: Light typography creates an elegant, modern feel aligned with award-winning agency sites. The generous letter-spacing and line-height ensure readability isn't compromised.

Purposeful Motion

Insight: All hover states and transitions use subtle transforms (translateY -4px, scale 1.02) and smooth timing (200-300ms). This creates polish without distraction—motion guides attention rather than demanding it.
