The Challenge

The Business Reality

Small businesses seeking professional websites face a harsh choice: cheap but generic DIY builders, expensive traditional agencies charging $20K-$50K with 3-6 month timelines, or variable-quality freelancers with uncertain communication and delivery.

What if AI-powered development could deliver agency-quality websites at freelancer prices with startup-level speed? Three projects would prove or disprove this hypothesis.

Development Methodology

AI-Powered Workflow

React combined with Sanity CMS emerged as optimal through real-world validation. Component-based architecture AI understands well. Server-side rendering for SEO. Fast page loads. Zero-config deployment to Vercel.

Sanity proved particularly valuable for client empowerment: non-technical clients editing content through intuitive interface, real-time collaboration, structured content preventing formatting mistakes, developer-friendly API integrating cleanly with React.
