SOLUTION: Separate every major decision into its own step.

Design Rationale

This brutalist, card-based approach prioritizes modularity and user control. Users can expand only the sections they care about, making the experience feel more like exploring a tool than reading a document. The high-contrast black/white/orange palette is bold and memorable, avoiding typical tech company aesthetics.

Concept 3:
Expandable Card Grid

Strategy

Transform case studies into an interactive grid of expandable cards. Users click to reveal content they're interested in, creating a more exploratory, tool-like experience.

Strengths

Highly scannable, users control their journey, brutalist aesthetic is distinctive and memorable, works well on tablets, feels interactive and engaging, modular structure is flexible.

Tradeoffs

Requires clicking to see content (less passive consumption), brutalist style may feel too aggressive for some projects, black/white/orange palette limits emotional range, grid layout can feel fragmented.
