About Page Design Mood Board

Three distinct visual directions for Michael Evans' About page, each exploring a different aesthetic approach while maintaining professional credibility and personal warmth.

Design Directions Overview

Direction 1: Minimal Elegance

Refined serif typography, generous whitespace, subtle animations. Feels like a well-designed book or upscale magazine.

  • Serif headings (Crimson Pro)
  • 90% whitespace, 10% content
  • Cream/ivory backgrounds
  • Subtle fade-in animations
  • Professional, approachable, timeless

Direction 2: Bold Brutalist

Dark, unconventional layout, monospace typography, high contrast. Feels like a developer's personal site or indie game UI.

  • Monospace fonts (JetBrains Mono)
  • Asymmetric grid layout
  • Neon accents on black
  • Sharp edges, no rounded corners
  • Technical, distinctive, memorable

Direction 3: Current Site Evolution

Dark theme with purple gradients, refined typography, familiar patterns. Natural evolution of existing brand.

  • Sans-serif (DM Sans, not Inter)
  • Purple gradient accents
  • Dark backgrounds (#050510)
  • Smooth transitions
  • Cohesive with existing portfolio

Design Rationale

Why Three Directions?

Each concept explores a fundamentally different approach to presenting Michael's story:

  • Minimal Elegance: Appeals to executive/enterprise clients who value sophistication and clarity
  • Bold Brutalist: Stands out in portfolio reviews, appeals to startups and creative teams
  • Current Evolution: Maintains brand consistency while elevating the existing aesthetic
