01

Virgin America Responsive Redesign

In 2013, I led the UX design and front-end architecture for Virgin America's groundbreaking responsive website redesign.

No major airline had successfully implemented a mobile-first booking experience. We changed that.

→
02

The Challenge

Airlines were notoriously slow to adopt modern web standards. Most booking flows required desktop computers and frustrated mobile users.

Conversion rates hovered below 2%, while mobile traffic grew 40% year-over-year with no mobile solution.

Virgin America needed to break industry patterns and create a delightful mobile experience.

→
03

The Solution

We designed a mobile-first responsive system that adapted seamlessly from 320px phones to 2560px desktops.

Key innovations: progressive disclosure for complex forms, gesture-based seat selection, real-time price updates that worked offline.

The entire booking flow could be completed on a smartphone with zero friction.

→
04
