Concept 3: Monochrome Fire
Immersive Modal + Scroll-Triggered Animations

Approach: Minimalist card grid for AI projects. Clicking opens full-screen modal with rich, immersive details. Case studies use scroll-snapped sections with animated transitions, like a presentation deck.

Aesthetic: Monochrome palette (grays/zinc) with single fire gradient accent (orange-red). Bold Manrope typography. Clean, confident, minimal.

Best for: Portfolios that want to make a bold statement. Design-forward professionals who value restraint and impact over decoration.

The Challenge

Airlines were notoriously slow to adopt modern web standards. Most booking flows required desktop computers and frustrated mobile users.

Conversion rates hovered below 2%, while mobile traffic grew 40% year-over-year with no mobile solution.

Virgin America needed to break industry patterns and create a delightful mobile experience.

The Solution

We designed a mobile-first responsive system that adapted seamlessly from 320px phones to 2560px desktops.

Key innovations: progressive disclosure for complex forms, gesture-based seat selection, real-time price updates that worked offline.

The entire booking flow could be completed on a smartphone with zero friction.

Concept 3 - Analysis

✓ Strengths

  • Bold, confident aesthetic that commands attention
  • Monochrome + single accent creates maximum visual impact
  • Full-screen modal provides immersive, distraction-free experience
  • Scroll-snapped sections control narrative pacing perfectly
  • Minimal design puts content first, doesn't compete with work
  • Most distinctive and memorable of the three concepts

⚠ Tradeoffs

  • Scroll-snap can feel restrictive - users can't stop mid-section
  • Full-screen modal blocks all other content (by design, but polarizing)
  • Minimal aesthetic may feel too sparse for some audiences
  • Single accent color limits visual variety across projects
  • Scroll-triggered animations require careful testing for accessibility
